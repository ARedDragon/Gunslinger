How To Play

1. Enter your outlaw name, then click Ranked Queue.
2. Matchmaking favors players near your rating, widening over time.
3. Pick one move each round. Only Shoot or Fanfire wins award points.
4. If both Shoot, a reaction duel starts: wait on red, click on green.
5. First to 3 points wins. Use coins for themes and cosmetics.
